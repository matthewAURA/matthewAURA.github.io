VEX Drivetrain Calculator

Helps you get the perfect drive configuration for your VEX robot.

Filter by the desired final speed of the robot (relative to a 1:1 100RPM robot with 4" wheels), the type of internal and external gearing, size of wheels and wheel configuration. The end product is a nice list of possible configurations that match the parameters you are looking for.
